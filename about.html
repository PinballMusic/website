About Pinball


    Who are we?

    Pinball is a streaming website exclusively for music and sounds alike. The team's page where you can see our staff is located right here.

    Pinball has convenient and simple functions to ensure that artists can spread their tracks through methods located on our site, and share them externally as well for exposure. Exposure is done most effectively for social media, such as Twitter and Facebook.

    With our one button to upload for and our simple interface, we strive to be user friendly and make decisions on behalf of the artists' and services' best interest. For more information about our values, see our values page.


    Where are we?

    Pinball can be found on:

    • YouTube
    • Twitter

    How do I contact you?

    To contact Pinball, you can use either Twitter or Email, however Email is preferred. We will try to answer generally all of our emails within 1-3 weeks of them being sent. For official and press purposes, you may also contact us at our email.


    How do I join the staff team?

    You can either be selected, or apply. Applications are located on this page for you to fill out, and they will automatically be addressed to the administration team. We only select staff ourselves without application when we believe they have shown significant responsibility, maturity, and dedication for the service. Do not ask to join the staff team unless you fill out an application.
