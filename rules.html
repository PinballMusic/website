Our Mission


Pinball is intended to be a music platform for all users to share and enjoy music, whether it be released by a label, or an individual user. We take precaution in ensuring that our community gets the best out of their Pinball experience, and we have these guidelines to ensure that users, labels, & content creators are respected alike:


Basic Etiquette

  • Please only post constructive comments, through any communication service provided on our platform.
  • Please do not harass or be disrespectful to any individuals, groups, or labels. We have no tolerance for any users that set out for bullying people.
  • Don’t impersonate labels, staff, or users. You may take the name of an artist, however you may not act as them on our platform.
  • Please do not share other users’ personal information unless they explicitly post it themselves. We also recommend that artists do not give out their address or phone number, and keep it to the preset contact platforms on profiles. Distribution of these personal media cover all of our website.
  • Please do not create novelty accounts for the purpose of a few times use, or cyber squat multiple names that won’t be used. This will only be enforced in the event that an artist requests a name of an account that is not used.
  • Do not post any hate speech or pornographic content.
