The Team.
The people who make this project possible.



Administrators

The administrators are essentially the operators of the service. These administrators manage the moderators, manage the website, and work on many projects in an effort to improve the platform. An administrator is marked by an orange name on the website. Hover over one to learn more about them!


Moderators

The moderators are the "enforcers" of Pinball. These moderators oversee the website to regulate everything; keeping users safe, enforce community guidelines and assist the administrators when needed. A moderator is marked by a green name on the website. You may apply for this position!
