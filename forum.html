    The Forum


    The forums are an external service hosted by ProBoards to have a more casual platform of discussion rather than the forums being on this Pinball website. The service, while officially connected to Pinball, is not our hosting, it still will have ground rules and the same staff as if the forum were hosted by here. As it's a slightly different format and design, and it may be confusing at times, you may email us questions on how to use it, or visit the support forums, also by ProBoards.


    Forum Rules

    When your account is registered with ProBoards, you are to obey all restrictions and guidelines from their official page. Disobeying these on our forum service will result in a termination, and if further action is required, forwarding to a ProBoards administrator. We take their guidelines very seriously, as they are also in line for user safety.

    Please consider the following on our Pinball forums:

  • Do not impersonate users, labels, groups, or staff members.
  • Maintain a calm, casual atmosphere. Be respectful.
  • Do not advertise or solicit external services not Pinball related.
  • Do not spam or abuse any website functions.
  • Do not abuse any forum exploits. Contact us.
  • Do not steal content and claim it as your own content.
  • Do not create "novelty" accounts that will be used only once.
