Core Values


Pinball has 10 basic courtesies in mind, and our values are operated on the structure of the community. We will always show unconditional appreciation for the ones who support us, and keep in check everything that needs to be. Our infrastructure is meant to be secured and responsibly handled.


The 10 Values

  • 1. Treating every user on the platform with respect & basic courtesy.
  • 2. Being transparent: if transparency isn't an option, being honest.
  • 3. Having freedom of speech and expression that doesn't infringe rules.
  • 4. Having the community be the main operator of Pinball.
  • 5. Actively responding to community ideas and thoughts.
  • 6. Giving thorough and consistent updates when needed.
  • 7. Providing high quality staff to cater to the site.
  • 8. Being open to trying new things out.
  • 9. Keeping user information safe and protected.
  • 10. Embrace other stances and opinions when we change.

If you have a question about any of our values, you can contact us and we are more than happy to elaborate on it. We want what's in the best interest for the community.
