Privacy Policy


Pinball records and stores data under all community accounts, under many different ways:


  • When your account is registered with us, your email is processed to verify your account. The verification process is solely performed by the user who requested it, and emails are stored securely in the database, being only visible to Administrators.
  • Adding details to your own account is at your own discretion. You are liable for all information put onto your own account, and this information is available to all site users and may be used in any data collection.
  • Your IP address that you register with is stored in the event of checking connections with other accounts. We use these to detect users that evade their account terminations or temporary bans.
  • Purchases in our store are processed not by us, but through Braintree. We do not have record in our database of sensitive information used to purchase premium packages.
  • Passwords by users are protected with a ~70 long hash, and SALT. No administrator may get access to your own account, and we do our best to protect the users of the community. Giving out your own password to other users is at your own risk.

When our privacy policy changes, we will inform you 30 days upon release in advance of the change. All of the information collected is for the sole purpose of improvement upon our service structure. Everything above is protected information, and we will enforce protection of them.


If you have questions, please contact us.
